Shimaura J.H.S student council

Student Council 19th Nisshin Geppo

1. Overview of student council

The Shimaura Junior High School Student Council was established in 2007, and the current student council president is Rentaro Kazaki, the 17th president.

2. History of the student council

2006 Start of student council establishment plan

2006 First student council members decided

2007 Student Council Established

2008 Shimaura Festival was held as a graduation project

2008 2nd term student council first meeting held

2009 Yotsuba class established

2011 The 2nd Shimaura Festival was held as a graduation project

2014 Air conditioner installed in student council room

2015 Completed installation of air conditioners in all classrooms and gymnasium

2017 Completed distribution of tablet devices to all students

2019 The 3rd Shimaura Festival was held as a graduation project

2020 Shortened classes and student council activities canceled due to the spread of coronavirus

2021 Student council activities resumed

3. Student council initiatives

The student council distributed tablets to each student (free of charge). You can use it even after graduation.

We install chairs for graduation ceremonies and decorations for entrance ceremonies. We also hold graduation commemorative events.

4. Notice from the student council

The student council is conducting a plastic bottle collection project. If you have plastic bottle waste, please
Please put it in the recycling box in front of the student council room.
